Singonyms 10

Are you able to maintain faith in being alive after you have experienced infatuation?
A sensation emanating from within myself is telling me:
“It is not my opinion that you have the requisite strength”

Cher - Believe
“Do you believe in life after love?
I can feel something inside me say
I really don’t think you’re strong enough”
